# WordPress Editorial Playbook

**Version:** 1.0  
**Status:** Platform Editorial Standard  
**Scope:** PiercingConnect content published on WordPress (reviews, guides, FAQ pages)

This playbook defines how verified PiercingConnect knowledge is published as canonical web articles on WordPress while preserving editorial integrity. WordPress is the **source-of-record destination** that social and video channels summarize—not the other way around.

It is subordinate to the PiercingConnect Editorial Style Guide.

---

## 1. Platform Purpose

WordPress hosts PiercingConnect’s authoritative, long-form editorial content: product reviews, aftercare guides, comparisons, and reference pages optimized for reading, citation, search discovery, and human review—not ephemeral social formats.

---

## 2. Audience Intent

Readers want complete, trustworthy answers they can read sequentially, bookmark, and share. They expect clear structure, honest limits, visible disclosures, and the ability to verify claims through source notes.

---

## 3. User Behavior

- Arrives via search, social link, or direct visit; scans headings before reading.
- Expects fast load, readable typography, and logical heading hierarchy.
- May read only one section (FAQ, limitations); each section must stand alone responsibly.
- Compares PiercingConnect tone to medical and commercial sites; trusts calm precision.
- Returns when updating aftercare routines; evergreen accuracy builds loyalty.

---

## 4. Content Goals

- Publish gold-standard articles that satisfy the Editorial Style Guide Gold Standard definition.
- Serve as the single canonical URL per topic for all platform adaptations.
- Preserve full evidence boundaries, source notes, affiliate disclosure, and review metadata.
- Enable responsible SEO through structure and accuracy—not keyword manipulation.
- Remain draft until human approval; never auto-publish unreviewed content.

---

## 5. Tone of Voice

Calm authority throughout. Educational before commercial. Plain language with correct terminology. Second person for practical instructions. No hype, fear, or influencer cadence. Match the constitution’s brand voice table in full.

---

## 6. Opening / Hook Rules

**Title:** Search-friendly and accurate; describes content honestly (not clickbait).

**Opening sections (Editorial Summary + Product Overview or equivalent):**
- Establish why the reader is here.
- State scope and evaluation method.
- Teach category context before product specifications.
- Set trust in the first 30 seconds of reading.
- Never open with affiliate links or “best product” claims unsupported by the review methodology.

H1 is the page title; H2 sections follow the editorial template for the content type.

---

## 7. Content Structure

**Product review (minimum):**
- Editorial Summary
- Product Overview
- Verified Formula
- Evidence and Guideline Alignment
- Potential Advantages
- Limitations and Uncertainties
- Who May Consider It
- Who Should Seek Professional Guidance
- Alternatives (fair, non-ranked or methodology-defined ranking)
- FAQ
- Final Verdict (when applicable)
- Source Notes
- Affiliate Disclosure

**Guides and explainers:** Problem → context → safe boundaries → escalation → sources → disclosure if commercial links present.

Use internal links to related canonical articles. FAQ answers must be substantive, not thin SEO blocks.

---

## 8. CTA Strategy

- Primary CTA: informed decision-making (“choose based on professional guidance and product facts”).
- Secondary: related internal links (alternatives, aftercare guides).
- Affiliate CTAs appear after educational content; never interrupt safety sections.
- No urgency, false scarcity, or outcome guarantees.
- Email or subscribe CTAs are optional and subordinate to reader safety content.

---

## 9. Evergreen Strategy

WordPress is the evergreen hub. All platform adaptations should link here.

- One canonical URL per topic; update in place for material corrections.
- Avoid date-stamped URLs for durable topics.
- Revision history documented internally; significant public corrections noted when appropriate per editorial policy.
- Retire or redirect pages that cannot be maintained accurately.
- Social and video assets must be updated when canonical articles change materially.

---

## 10. News / Trend Strategy

Publish timely WordPress posts only for verified developments: product formulation changes, safety advisories, guidance updates, or materially new evidence in the source record.

- Clearly label news/update posts in title or intro.
- Link from news post to related evergreen canonical page.
- After the news cycle, merge durable content into the evergreen article and consolidate URLs if needed (redirect, not duplicate).
- Do not publish trend reactive posts without verified sourcing.

---

## 11. Affiliate Integration Rules

- Affiliate Disclosure section conspicuous and plain-language on every page with affiliate links.
- Disclosure near top for long pages when practical.
- Product verdicts independent of commission; limitations stated even for affiliate products.
- Comparison tables neutral unless explicit methodology and criteria are declared in-article.
- Suitability language must not contradict limitations sections.

---

## 12. Things to Avoid

- Publishing draft or AI-unverified content.
- Keyword stuffing, doorway pages, or duplicate near-identical URLs.
- Bait-and-switch titles or meta descriptions.
- Removing limitations to improve conversion.
- Hiding disclosures below unrelated content.
- Medical diagnosis or treatment instructions.
- Guaranteed healing language.
- Broken or placeholder source references.

---

## 13. Success Metrics

- Organic search impressions and CTR with honest titles (quality match).
- Time on page and scroll depth on limitations and FAQ sections.
- Return visitor rate to PiercingConnect articles.
- Internal link paths (readers finding related guides).
- Human review approval rate and time-to-correction for errors.
- Affiliate click-through **relative to** educational engagement—not as primary success alone.
- Zero tolerance tracking: safety-related corrections, disclosure omissions, unpublished draft leaks.

---

## 14. Approval Checklist

Use the Editorial Style Guide Publishing Checklist in full, plus WordPress-specific items:

- [ ] Canonical URL assigned; no unnecessary duplicate topic pages.
- [ ] Title, meta description, and H1 aligned and non-deceptive.
- [ ] All required review sections present for content type.
- [ ] Source Notes complete; no placeholders.
- [ ] Affiliate Disclosure visible where required.
- [ ] Final Verdict (if used) matches evidence and limitations sections.
- [ ] Internal links functional and relevant.
- [ ] Publish status is **draft** until human final approval (production standard).
- [ ] Platform adaptations notified to update if this canonical page changed materially.
- [ ] Human editorial approval recorded before live publish.
