## Editorial Summary

Steri-Wash Sterile Saline Piercing Spray / Wash is a **sterile saline piercing spray-mist** sold on the official Steri-Wash storefront in **3 oz**, **4.25 oz**, and **8 oz** sizes. Official features list **no drugs or preservatives**, **360° dispensing**, sterility maintained throughout canister life, and spray ingredients as **Water & Sodium Chloride**. (`official-steri-wash`)

This review evaluates Steri-Wash against **declared criteria**—ingredient transparency, SKU-specific concentration evidence, sterility/preservative claims, format, and overlap or gaps versus Association of Professional Piercers (APP) aftercare guidance. It is **not** a ranked “best saline” verdict and does **not** guarantee healing outcomes.

**Important evidence boundary (SKU-scoped):** Official **4.25 oz** product back label evidence lists **Purified Water** and **0.9% USP NaCl** and describes an **isotonic** solution. That **0.9%** claim is **not** verified for **3 oz** or **8 oz** sizes on materials consulted—do not treat it as a full-line fact. For category literacy, start with the [Sterile Saline Complete Guide](#related-guides) (`pc-hub-sterile-saline-complete-guide`). (`app-aftercare`, `official-steri-wash`)

This article is informational, not medical advice.

## Evidence and Guideline Alignment

### Professional guidance (category layer) — `app-aftercare`

APP Suggested Aftercare for Body Piercings strongly encourages **sterile saline labeled for wound wash**, with ingredients listing **0.9% sodium chloride** as the only ingredient (sometimes purified water). APP advises against DIY sea-salt mixes, harsh cleaners, over-cleaning, and using contact-lens saline, nasal spray, or eye drops. APP does **not** endorse any specific brand. (`app-aftercare`)

- Wholesale **“.09%”** page copy is **not** used here as concentration proof. (`steri-wash-4-25-page-dot-09-text`)  
- **No verified healing or infection-prevention outcomes.** (`steri-wash-healing-claims`)  
- **Oral piercing suitability** not specified on pages consulted. (`steri-wash-recommended-for-oral`)  
- **No universal rinse schedule** in APP guidance; follow piercer and on-pack directions for the SKU purchased. (`app-aftercare`)  
- **8 oz** wholesale use language includes burns, wounds, piercings, and moistening dressings—treat as manufacturer use framing, not a substitute for piercer/medical advice. (`official-steri-wash`)  
- **3 oz** “medical grade / hospitals” framing is manufacturer marketing.  
- Individual responses vary; saline cannot compensate for unsuitable jewelry or incorrect aftercare practice.

## FAQ

**Q: Is Steri-Wash 0.9% sodium chloride?**  
A: **Yes for the verified 4.25 oz back label** (Purified Water, 0.9% USP NaCl; isotonic label wording). **Not verified** here for **3 oz** or **8 oz**. Check the can you buy. Do not rely on wholesale “.09%” marketing copy as proof. (`official-steri-wash`)

**Q: How often should I use it?**  
A: APP guidance does not give a fixed universal daily schedule for all piercings. Follow your piercer and the directions on the specific Steri-Wash SKU. (`app-aftercare`)

**Q: Is it the same as APP-preferred packaged sterile saline?**  
A: It overlaps on packaged sterile saline, water + sodium chloride, no drugs/preservatives, and spray use. **0.9%** is documented on the **4.25 oz** label; full-line concentration match is **unresolved**. APP does not endorse brands. (`app-aftercare`, `official-steri-wash`)

**Q: Can I use homemade salt water instead?**  
A: APP suggested aftercare no longer recommends DIY sea-salt mixes; they are commonly too salty and may over-dry the piercing. Prefer packaged sterile saline wound wash where available. (`app-aftercare`)

**Q: What if I have irritation or worry about infection?**  
A: Do not self-diagnose from an article. Contact a piercer or healthcare provider for concerning or worsening symptoms.
