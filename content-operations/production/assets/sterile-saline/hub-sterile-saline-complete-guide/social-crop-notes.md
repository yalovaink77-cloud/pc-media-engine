# Social Crop Notes — Hub: Sterile Saline Complete Guide

## Master asset

- **Aspect:** 16:9
- **Recommended export:** 1920×1080 px (JPEG quality 85–90, or WebP)
- **WordPress role:** Featured image (hero), Open Graph default when no custom OG image set

---

## 16:9 hero (featured image)

- **Focal point:** Generic saline spray can + comparison graphic cluster at horizontal center
- **Top/bottom margin:** Leave 10% clear for theme title overlay if Kadence adds headline band
- **Horizon:** Table surface line at lower third; avoid cropping through product base
- **Text-safe zones:** Lower-left and upper-right corners often used by social cards—keep low-detail negative space there if possible

---

## 1:1 square crop (social / Pinterest / Instagram feed)

- **Crop from:** Center of 16:9 master
- **Export:** 1080×1080 px minimum (1200×1200 for Pinterest)
- **Keep in frame:** Spray can and at least one comparison element (salt jar OR wound-wash label cue)
- **May crop out:** Far-left or far-right negative space; optional small badge if redundant in square format
- **Do not crop:** Through the middle of the spray can or through comparison icons
